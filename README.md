# 🚗 HNG Ride Data Analysis Project

## 📊 Overview
This project presents an end-to-end data analysis of a ride-hailing platform (HNG Ride), covering operations from June 2021 to December 2024.

The goal was to answer key business questions around revenue trends, rider retention, driver performance, and operational efficiency using SQL.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze ride and payment data to uncover business insights
- Evaluate rider retention and engagement patterns
- Assess driver performance and incentive structures
- Identify revenue trends and operational inefficiencies

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **SQL (PostgreSQL)** – Data cleaning & analysis  
- **Excel/CSV** – Raw dataset handling

## 📈 Key Insights

### 1. Revenue Trends
- Revenue peaked in 2022 and declined slightly in 2023–2024
- Indicates potential market saturation or operational inefficiencies

### 2. Rider Retention
- 1,815 riders from 2021 remained active in 2024
- Strong customer loyalty and retention performance

### 3. Cancellation Rates
- ~21% cancellation rate across all cities
- Major operational issue impacting revenue and user experience

### 4. Driver Utilization
- Most drivers average fewer than 2 rides per month
- Indicates underutilization and possible supply-demand imbalance

### 5. Bonus Program Inefficiency
- Only 1 driver qualified for incentives
- Suggests overly strict and demotivating criteria
